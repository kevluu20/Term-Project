FirstGenAid ™ Project Proposal

What is your website about?

My website is going to be about helping first generation students navigate the complicated college process. This will give struggling students in high school a resource that they can go to for tips, important information, and steps on how to tackle the college applications. Being a first generation student myself, I recieved a lot of help from teachers and friends. I want to help other students now.

What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)

The objective of my website is to help first generation students who are applying for colleges without fully knowing what has to get done. This site is important because it is meant to assist, inspire, and prepare students for the college application process and college itself.

Who is your target audience set?

My target audience is primarily for first generation high school students who are looking into applying for college, but don't have the resources to help them through the process.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

The college application process has a bunch of different components that are all crucial for attending college. Some of the content will include:

  • Financial Aid
  • Common Application
  • Testing
  • Scholarships
