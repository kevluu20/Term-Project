Scholarship Search

Apply for Scholarships

Applying for scholarships is tedious, long, and stressful. However, the feeling you get if you recieve a scholarship is like no other. It lifts some pressure from your shoulders and allows you to focus more on school rather than the tuition that comes with it. Even if you believe the scholarship is too competitve to get the it, it does not hurt to just apply and see what happens. Look at the requirements for the scholarship, deadlines, and qualifications. Make sure that you are starting the scholarship search early because there will be many students looking to take advantage of those opportunities too.

Also, it is recommended to talk to your school counselors about local scholarships that are available and if there are applications for those scolarships. Don't wait until the last minute to start applying or you will hurt your chances of being considered. There are scholarships everywhere that are given to students. It is your job to do your research and find them. It is guarenteed that there is a scholarship that fits you.

Scholarship Do's

  1. Start Looking For Scholarships Early

    2. The early you get started with the scholarship search process, the less stressful you will be later on. This allows you to take your time, read over your application carefully, and edit.

  2. Apply For Many Different Scholarships

    3. There are many different types of scholarships available for students. It could be academically-related, financially-related, for athletics, art, and much more. Try to diversify the types of scholarships you apply for and see which ones relate to you to maximize your chances of recieving an award.

  3. Include Recommendation Letters

    4. Recommendation letters are always a great reference to have for the committee who are deciding who deserves the scholarship the most. It gives them a sense of how well you are as a student and person. In addition, it shows them that other people believe in you and will vouch for you.

Scholarship Don'ts

  1. Don't Get Discouraged If You Don't Recieve Awards

    2. Remember that students from all over are applying for the same scholarships that you are. If you don't win one, don't get discouraged. Keep applying so that there is a chance of success. Giving up will only make getting a scholarship impossible.

  2. Don't Skip Scholarships That Offer Less Money

    3. Scholarships are scholarships and money is money. Just because some awards are lower than other doesn't mean that they aren't worth it. You will be surprised to see that money adds up and every dollar matters when it comes to paying for college.

  3. Don't Rush The Application

    4. The application is meant to show people who you are and why you deserve the scholarship. If you procratinate and rush through the application just to submit it, you will find that you are less likely to recieve an award. Scholarships are competitive and it is crucial that you portray your best self.

Tips For Getting Scholarships

  1. Apply Early And Often

  2. Keep Track Of Applications And Be Organized

  3. Look Out For Scams
