About Us

Location

We empower students who are the first in their family to attend college to achieve their dreams.

The idea of this site drew inspiration from the diverse city of Malden, MA, located within the Middlesex County. In Malden, there are a lot of first-generation students that are not sure what to do after high school. Some students have no idea what applying for college is like and how to submit all the necessary forms to be considered when applying. The college application process is one that is confusing, stressful, and time consuming. There are millions of first generation students in the United States who need assistance. Too many high school students are not living up to their full potential because they are not educated about college.

FirstGenAid is here to help students in need. We provide students with financial aid advice, given that college nowadays is very expensive. We also provide sources to scholarships, testing tips for the SAT and ACT, review the requirements for the Common Application, and much more. We hope that our site gives value to our audience and would encourage people to share this site or what you learned from it with peers who also are struggling and lost with the college process.
